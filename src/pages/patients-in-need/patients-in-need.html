Food Insecurity

  • TWO-QUESTION survey from Hager, et al. 2010

    • Within the past 12 months did you worry whether your food would
      run out before you got money to buy more?

    • Within the past 12 months, was there a time when the food you
      bought didn't last and you didn't have money to buy more?

    • For more information, check out the publication:
      http://pediatrics.aappublications.org/content/126/1/e26.full.html

    • See the document below

  • Do you need help in getting food by the end of the month? IF YES,
    would you like help with this (WE CARE)?
