Texas 2-1-1

Texas 2-1-1 is a searchable statewide list of resources.

Call 2-1-1 or check out their website for a nice overview of resources on employment opportunities, food insecurity, housing needs, health care (including access to health insurance, primary and specialty care), financial and legal aid, child care and early education, veterans support services, and aging and disability resources.
