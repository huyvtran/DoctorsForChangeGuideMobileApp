Dental Services Information

  • 27.3% of children in Harris County did not have dental insurance in 20101.

  • 15.3% of parents delayed dental care for their children due to cost or lack of insurance1.

  • 82.3% of children ages 2-17 in the US had a dental visit in the last year, compared to 61.6% of adults in the US2.

  • Over 40% of poor adults (20 years and older) have at least one untreated decayed tooth compared to 16% of non-poor adults2.
