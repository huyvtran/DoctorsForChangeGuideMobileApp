Domestic Violence Information

  • On average, 24 people per minute are victims of rape, physical violence, or stalking by an intimate partner in the U.S., based on a survey conducted by the CDC in 20101.

  • 1 in 4 women have been the victim of severe physical violence by an intimate partner, while 1 in 7 men have experienced the same1.

  • 72.9% of family violence in Texas in 2012 was committed against women2.
